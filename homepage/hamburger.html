Hamburgers!

just a simple page about hamburgers

Step Instructions
1

In a large bowl, massage the ground beef until it is sticky and leaves a residue on the bowl.
2

Heat up your stainless steel or cast iron pan until it is hot enough for water to form a ball and skate around the pan
3

Turn down the pan to medium heat
4

Place a 1/3 cup sized ball of hamburger into the pan and let it sear until it can be easily moved in the pan
5

Squish down the burger to the desired thickness with a spatula and cook until dark brown on the bottom
6

Flip the burger and cook until the internal temperature is at least 160 F
7 Serve!
